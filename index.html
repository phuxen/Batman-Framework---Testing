Burberry loves Printemps

Bespoke digital interactive

22 August to 20 October 2013

Burberry, the iconic British brand, brings the energy of London to the heart of Paris in Printemps. Enter a world of eclectic weather, distinctive style and historic architecture, set to the rhythm of the after-dark live music scene.

Enjoy our Burberry Loves PRINTEMPS pop-up store, bringing London culture to life against a backdrop of cityscapes and innovative digital screens. Discover the exclusive collection, featuring leather, studs, denim and accessories, directly inspired by Britain’s music culture.

Celebrate the iconic Burberry trench coat with the dancing mannequins in the Atrium. For the first time in Paris, Burberry and Printemps invite you to participate in Art of the Trench, the brand’s ever-evolving street-style photographic project.
