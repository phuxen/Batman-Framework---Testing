Paul Huxen
Some say he's a 'Pusher of pixels', some say he's the 'Ed Norton of design', others say he fucks about for a living…

Paul Huxen is a bearded & (usually) bespectacled designer who, when not quaffing copious amounts of tea, is to found designing interfaces for Burberry in jolly London town. He also uses his spare time to build a collection of fonts through Kraken Foundry.
